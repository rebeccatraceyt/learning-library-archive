Stripe Errors

401 or 403 error - set your port to public!

A webhook cannot be received unless your site is publicly accessible. This is not an issue with your deployed app, but is an extra step is required when testing in your local IDE.

First, run your local server from the terminal:

user@ci-os /home/user (main) $ python3 manage.py runserver

Then, you can set the server port by either:

1 Clicking "Make Public" from the popup:
2 By accessing your list of available ports and clicking the unlock button:

Once you have set your port to public, make sure to refresh your page. Then, you are ready to test your webhooks!
