Checklist:

Removing migrations and resetting your database

Note that this should be a last resort when fixing Django migration or database issues:
python3 manage.py makemigrations --dry-run
python3 manage.py makemigrations
python3 manage.py migrate --plan
python3 manage.py migrate
python3 manage.py createsuperuser
python3 manage.py loaddata file_name.json
python3 manage.py runserver
Your SQLite Database and migrations have been successfully reset!
